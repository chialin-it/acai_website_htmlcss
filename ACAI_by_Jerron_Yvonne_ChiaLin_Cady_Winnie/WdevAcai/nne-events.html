Upcoming Events

Celebrate SG50 with ACAI

Singaore Botanic Gardens has just became is the country’s first UNESCO Heritage Site, joining the ranks of iconic landmarks such as Cambodia’s Angkor Wat and The Great Wall of China.

Join ACAI by bringing your pets to Singapore Botanic Garden and celebrate SG50 with us! Many fun games and bonding activities to enjoy with your family and pets!

Venue: Singapore Botanic Gardens, Shaw Foundation Symphony Stage Date & Time: 1 August 2015, 5pm

Join ACAI at Pet Expo 2015

Pet Expo 2015, Singapore's largest PET-educational fair is back from 10-12 August 2015 at Singapore Expo Hall 8. The show is an all-encompassing event that caters to the gamut of needs of pets and pet owners. Pet owners and animal lovers can look forward to bond with your beloved pets over the weekend through activities like workshops, seminars, contests and competitions.

SPCA will be participating in this fun-filled event where we will feature our cute and cuddly smallies up for adoption, our brand new merchandise for sale and our Animal Ambassadors will be up to grab, if you want to know anything and everything on animal welfare.

For the first time in Singapore, Pet Expo will be partnering with AVA to introduce a speed dating adoption event! This March, we will be presenting to you Singapore’s most eligible bachelors and bachelorettes! Looking for a soulmate with soft brown hair and ocean blue eyes? Make your way to AVA booth on Day 2 and Day 3 of Pet Expo to meet these gorgeous furry friends and you may just cross eyes with the next love of your life!

Past Events

Say NO To Shark Fin Soup Campaign 2014

Singapore is one of the largest shark fin trading nation. Unfortunately, there is only one small sustainable shark fishery in global terms, and the development of sustainable fisheries for sharks is likely to be far too slow to effectively conserve shark populations in most parts of the world.

Join ACAI to pledge and take off Shark Fin Soup off the menu in Singapore!

ACAI Celebrates World Animal Day 2013

World Animal Day is here again! This year, we collaborate with the Agri-Food & Veterinary Authority of Singapore (AVA) and nine other local animal welfare groups to campaign for animal welfare. The three-day celebration will be held at the basement Atrium of United Square, from 13 September 2013 (Friday) to 15 September 2013 (Sunday). World Animal Day has always been the event of the year for us, and we can’t wait to take the celebrations into the mall!

This year’s theme, “Friends for Life”, conveys a clear message. Our pets are not commodities or objects that can be replaced or abandoned. They are our best friends and family members, and should be given the respect and love that our friends and family deserve.

Join us at United Square as we celebrate World Animal Day 2013!
