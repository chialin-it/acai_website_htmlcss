Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How long has ACAI been around?
ACAI was founded in 2013 by a group of 5 Singaporeans members, and registered as a society on the 13th of July 2013 (Registration No.: 0999/2013).
Are you a registered charity?
Yes. ACAI is registered under the Charities Act and registered as an Institution of Public Character (Registration No.: IPC000999).
Is ACAI an international charity?
ACAI is a Singapore-based charity, founded by Singaporeans. Our work is concentrated in Singapore and Southeast Asia.
Can I call you if I find a wild animal in trouble?
Yes definitely! You can call the ACAI Wilddtfe Rescue Hotline on +65 6586 8686 if you find a native wild animal or abandoned exotic wild animals in need of help, or if you know of anyone keeping prohibited wild animals as pets or of any shops or traders illegally selling wild animals or wild animal parts.
Do you rescue dogs and cats?
Yes. Our focus is mainly wildlife issues and on industrialised animal cruelty issues but we also try our very best to help the domestic pets in Singapore.
How can I help ACAI?
You can help in many ways: By volunteering with us, spreading the word about our work, supporting our events and contributing financially. You can find more about our volunteer opportunities on our Support Us pages and our events are listed on our News & Events page. Making a monthly donation is one of the best ways to help ACAI, as your regular gift allows us to plan, maintain and increase our long-term projects with confidence, safe in the knowledge that the funds will be there in the future.
How can I keep up to date with the latest news and happenings at ACAI?
You can sign up for our monthly e-newsletter under Support Us pages.
Where does my donation go to?
The majority of the funds we raise go directly to the animals. For the financial year 2013/14, at least 91% of funds raised went directly to our animal protection programmes. We try to keep our running costs to a minimum. Our accounts are independently audited by Parker Randall Certified Public Accountants.
Do I get tax exemption for my donation?
Yes! ACAI has the Institute of Public Character (IPC) status. This means that donations to ACAI qualify for 250% tax exemption. For example, if you donate $100 to ACAI, $250 will be deducted from your taxable income.
